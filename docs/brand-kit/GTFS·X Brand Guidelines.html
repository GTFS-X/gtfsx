01Identity

Logo system

The logo is a wordmark plus a coral square containing a chunky white X. The square reads as both a literal X-marks-the-spot pin and a stand-in for the letter X in the name — one mark doing two jobs.

Naming

In written form, write the brand as GTFS·X (with a middle dot) — not "GTFS X" or "GTFSX". The middle dot is what the box represents in the lockup, so the written form mirrors the visual form. Speak it as "G-T-F-S X".

✓ Written form
GTFS·X
✗ Avoid
GTFS X  ·  GTFSX  ·  gtfsx  ·  GTFS-X

The middle dot character is U+00B7 — on macOS press Opt+Shift+9, or copy from this doc. The domain remains gtfsx.dev (no dot in URLs).

Primary lockup

The default expression of the brand. Use this everywhere unless space forces you into the stacked or mark-only variants. The box is slightly taller than the wordmark (24% overhang) and vertically centered, with a 0.13em visual gap to the wordmark.

On white

Stacked & mark-only

For square or compact contexts — app icons, social avatars, favicons, presentation footers. The mark alone is also the brand at small sizes.

Stacked — square contexts
Mark only — favicons, icons

Clear space

Give the lockup room. Reserve a margin equal to the height of the box (×) on every side. Don't crowd the mark with other elements inside this zone.

× ×

Minimum size

Pixel-tuned mark exports exist for every favicon step. Below 16px, the X loses legibility — don't go smaller. For print, never use the mark below 12 mm wide.

Do & don't

✓ Do
Use the lockup at the correct proportions. Box matches cap height with slight overhang, 0.18em gap.
✗ Don't
GTFS
Recolor the mark to off-brand palettes. Coral, dark brown, or cream only.
✓ Do
Pronounce it "G-T-F-S X." The bullet is read as a pause, not a "dot."
✗ Don't
GTFS
Stretch, skew, or otherwise distort the box. The X stays square.

03Type

Typography

Three faces. Nunito for display & UI weight, Inter for body, JetBrains Mono for code, times, and IDs.

Aa
Nunito · headlines, buttons, the wordmark
Variable, geometric, slightly rounded. Use weights 700–900 only. Available locally — never load over CDN in production.
Aa
Inter · body, form labels, microcopy
Variable. Use weights 400 / 500 / 600 / 700. Tabular numerals for any data column.
Aa
JetBrains Mono · times, IDs, file paths
For HH:MM stops in timetables, route_id / stop_id, file references in docs.

04Words

Voice & tone

GTFS·X is a tool for transit planners who care about their craft. Talk to them like a peer, not a marketer.

Tone axis
Plainspoken, not buzzy
We say "build a feed", not "leverage transit data infrastructure."
Tone axis
Specific, not generic
We say "24 trips on weekdays", not "many trips daily."
Tone axis
Warm, not corporate
We say "Saved", not "Persistence successful."
Tone axis
Confident, not boastful
We say "Free and open source", not "The world's leading GTFS platform."
